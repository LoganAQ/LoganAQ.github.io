Overview

Description: This course is part of a financial literacy curriculum. The overall curriculum addresses topics, such as credit, taxes, investing, and mortgages. This course focuses on mortgage programs, specifically the difference betweeen conventional and non-conventional mortgages. It was created in Articulate Rise.


Learning Objectives:

  1. Describe the difference between conventional and non-conventional mortgages.
  2. Explain the criteria lenders and government sponsored enterprises use for loan qualification.
